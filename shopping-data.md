# This week's dinners and shopping list

Household: 1 adult plus 3 teens (12, 14, 16). Four dinners, Thursday through Sunday, leftovers into Monday. Style is simple, whole-ingredient, leaning Latin American, as cheap as possible. Store is WinCo, with a bias toward bulk bins.

## Dinner plan

| Night | Main | Kid backup |
|-------|------|------------|
| Thu | Garlic butter shrimp over rice | Mac and cheese + chicken nuggets |
| Fri | Ropa Vieja (Cuban shredded beef), rice & beans | Spaghetti with red sauce |
| Sat | Instant Pot carnitas, rice and beans | Spaghetti |
| Sun | Moros y Cristianos with leftover carnitas | Leftover spaghetti or nuggets |

Two teens who hate seafood get mac and cheese + nuggets Thursday. Ropa Vieja and carnitas both feed 4+ and produce good leftovers.

## Recipe sources (cross-referenced, not AI-invented)

- **Garlic butter shrimp** — butter, garlic, shrimp, lime, cilantro, rice. No source needed.
- **Ropa Vieja** — simplified from [The Sofrito Project](https://www.sofritoproject.com/recipes/2019/2/12/ropa-vieja). Chuck roast, onion, bell peppers, garlic, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, cumin, oregano, bay leaf, white vinegar.
- **Carnitas** — [Nom Nom Paleo Instant Pot Carnitas](https://nomnompaleo.com/instant-pot-pressure-cooker-carnitas). Pork shoulder, orange, garlic, onion, cumin, oregano, bay leaf, salt.
- **Moros y Cristianos** — [Three Guys From Miami / iCuban](https://icuban.com/food/moros_y_cristianos.html) (on the web since 1996). Black beans, onion, green pepper, garlic, cumin, oregano, bay leaf, white vinegar, tomato sauce, chicken stock, rice.
